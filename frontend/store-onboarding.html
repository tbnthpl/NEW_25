Store Onboarding

Add and maintain bank store master. Stores must be onboarded before Finacle upload and Store Mapping.

Bulk Upload (Admin Only)

Upload Excel (.xlsx or .xls) with columns: bank_store_code, effective_from, store_name, pickup_type (BEAT/CALL), customer_id, customer_name, account_no, sol_id. For BEAT: daily_pickup_limit, monthly_bank_charge (or fixed_charge), vendor_charge (₹/month). For CALL: call_included_pickups, call_monthly_bank_charge, call_additional_bank_per_pickup, call_vendor_pay_per_pickup. Optional: waiver_percentage, waiver_cap_amount, waiver_cap_from, waiver_cap_to.
